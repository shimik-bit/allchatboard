# 🚀 Quickstart - GroupGuard in 5 minutes

This guide walks you from registration to having the bot protecting your first WhatsApp group.

---

## Step 1: Connect AllChat (1 minute)

Before we start, you need to connect your AllChat account:

1. Go to **Settings** ← **WhatsApp connections**
2. Click **"Connect AllChat account"**
3. Enter the API Key from [allchat.co.il](https://allchat.co.il)
4. Confirm the connection

> 💡 Don't have an AllChat account yet? [Create one here](https://allchat.co.il) — there's a free plan to start.

---

## Step 2: Add the bot to a group (1 minute)

1. Open the group in WhatsApp
2. Click the group name ← **Add participants**
3. Add the **bot's number** (you'll find it in the GroupGuard dashboard)
4. Send any message in the group so the bot "sees" it

---

## Step 3: Enable GroupGuard on the group (30 seconds)

1. Back in TaskFlow ← **GroupGuard** ← **Groups** tab
2. You'll see the new group in the list (if not, click **"Scan groups"**)
3. Toggle the switch next to the group name ⚡
4. Done!

---

## Step 4: (Optional) Make the bot an admin

So the bot can **delete messages** and **remove spammers** automatically:

1. In the group ← click the bot's name
2. **Make admin**
3. Give it permissions:
   - ✅ Remove participants
   - ✅ Delete messages

> ⚠️ Without admin permissions the bot still works — but it'll only detect and alert. It can't act on its own.

---

## Step 5: Test (30 seconds)

1. Ask a friend to write a message in the group
2. Back in TaskFlow ← **GroupGuard** ← **Group Members** tab
3. You'll see them appear in the list within a minute

🎉 **That's it! GroupGuard is live and working.**

## Common issues

**Group not showing in the list?**
- Make sure the bot is actually in the group
- Send one message in the group
- Click **"Scan groups"** in the **Groups** tab

**Bot not responding to spam?**
- Make sure the bot is an admin in the group
- Check that the group's toggle is on
- See [FAQ](./08-faq.md)
