# 📊 GroupGuard Dashboard - What every number means

The dashboard is the first page you'll see in GroupGuard. It shows the real-time state of **all** your groups.

---

## Top metric cards

### 👥 Active members
The number of unique people who spoke in your groups in the last 30 days.

> 💡 **Why does this matter?** It shows how "alive" the group really is — not how many people are in it, but how many actually write.

### 💬 Messages today
Total messages that passed through the bot since the start of today.

### 🛡️ Protection actions
How many times the bot acted today (deletions, removals, warnings).

### 🚨 Spammers detected
Number of users that have been flagged as spammers by the system.

---

## Weekly activity chart

Shows the **3 most active groups** from the past week:
- **Blue** — regular messages
- **Red** — messages flagged as spam
- **Green** — new members who joined

> 💡 **How to read it:** If you see a sharp red spike — there was probably a spam attack. Click the chart to drill down.

---

## Recent events list

The 10 most recent bot actions across all your groups:

| Icon | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 🚫 | Member removed |
| 🗑️ | Message deleted |
| ⚠️ | Warning issued |
| ➕ | New member joined |
| 🔍 | Suspicious user detected |

---

## Quick filters

Above the dashboard there are filters that change what you see:

- **Time range:** Today / 7 days / 30 days / 90 days
- **Specific group:** All groups / one group
- **Activity type:** All / spam only / new members only

---

## Tips for reading the dashboard correctly

✅ **Healthy pace** = steady daily messages, few protection actions
⚠️ **Heads up** = sudden spike in detected spammers — check the group
🚨 **Problem** = more protection actions than regular messages = active spam attack
