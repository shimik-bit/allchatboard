# 🛡️ GroupGuard - WhatsApp Group Management Guide

Welcome to **GroupGuard** by TaskFlow AI - the smartest tool for managing and filtering WhatsApp groups.

## Why GroupGuard?

Professional WhatsApp groups are gold — but spammers, bots, and trolls can ruin them in a day. GroupGuard does the quiet work for you:

- 🤖 **Detects spammers automatically** and removes them
- 👥 **Builds member profiles** (profession, field, activity)
- 🌍 **Blocks suspicious country prefixes**
- 🚨 **Alerts admins** about suspicious activity
- 📊 **Shows statistics** — who writes, when, and how much

---

## Getting started?

### In short (3 steps)
1. **Connect AllChat** to TaskFlow (if you haven't already)
2. **Add the bot** to your WhatsApp group
3. **Enable GroupGuard** on the group from the dashboard

That's it! The system will start learning and acting automatically.

> 💡 **Tip:** The bot works without admin permissions (collects profiles, shows statistics), but to **delete messages** or **remove spammers** — it must be an admin in the group.

## Permission levels

GroupGuard works in two modes, and you choose:

### 🔓 **Without admin permissions**
- ✅ Automatic member profiles
- ✅ Search by profession / field
- ✅ Dashboard and statistics
- ✅ Full activity log
- ✅ Tag admins on spam

### 🛡️ **With admin permissions** (everything above +)
- ✅ Automatic warnings in chat
- ✅ Delete spam messages
- ✅ Auto-remove spammers
- ✅ Block country prefixes
- ✅ Global spammer database

> 💡 **You can start basic and make the bot an admin later.** The features will activate automatically.
